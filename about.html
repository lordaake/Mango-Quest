Inside mango quest:
The ultimate mango hunt

Mango Quest is not just another travel blog; it's an immersive journey dedicated to the pursuit of the world's most delectable mangoes. With a team of dedicated adventurers at its helm, this blog documents global expeditions that are as rich in flavor as they are in culture and thrill.


More than merely cataloging different types of fruit, Mango Quest delves into the stories, histories, and traditions surrounding each unique mango variety. From the mystical forests of Bali to the enigmatic Bermuda Triangle, we venture into some of the planet's most untouched terrains and ancient civilizations to discover mangoes that tantalize the palate and ignite the imagination.


Our exploration team consists of Tord, the Arctic-raised leader whose first taste of mango in India launched a lifelong passion; Leo, a 30-year-old botanist whose scientific approach adds a layer of depth to our quests; and Albert, a septuagenarian whose wealth of travel experiences adds a seasoning of wisdom and cultural knowledge to each adventure.


Through compelling narratives, striking photography, and firsthand experiences, our blog captures the essence of each extraordinary mango hunt. Join us as we traverse uncharted landscapes, unearth hidden gems, and celebrate the universal language of flavor. Mango Quest is not just a blog; it's your passport to the world of exotic tastes and untold stories. Welcome aboard!

Meet our team of adventurers

Tord, The Leader
Born and raised amidst the snow-covered fjords of Norway, Tord was the epitome of an Arctic explorer, comfortable in the ice and chill of the far north. However, his life took a flavorful turn during a transformative trip to India. There, in a bustling local market, he experienced the unforgettable taste of his first mango. Tord knew he had found a new mission in life: to explore the world in search of the most unique and captivating mango varieties.
As the group’s leader, Tord combines his innate sense of direction with an unquenchable thirst for adventure. His roots are in the frigid Arctic, but his heart now belongs to the tropical realms he explores with his team.
Leo, The Botanist
Growing up in the sprawling cityscape, Leo was always an odd one out with his nose buried in plant biology books. At the age of 30, he had already traveled through various ecosystems for his research. It was a rather academic life until he crossed paths with Tord and found a new way to channel his botanical expertise—through the exhilarating world of mango hunting. Intrigued by the idea of uncovering rare and exotic varieties of mangoes, Leo didn't think twice before joining the expedition.
As the team's go-to plant expert, Leo brings a scientific rigor to the adventure. His skills aren't just confined to laboratories and academic journals; he's equally at home slicing through dense foliage with a machete as he is examining mango samples under a microscope.
Albert, The Globe-Trotter
Albert has spent seven decades on this Earth, and he's filled those years with a lifetime of travel and adventure. From the ancient pyramids of Egypt to the secluded monasteries of Bhutan, Albert has seen it all. Just when he thought his years of adventuring were drawing to a close, he met Tord and discovered a whole new world of excitement in mango hunting. For Albert, joining the team was not just another chapter in his adventurous life but also a validation of his belief that exploration knows no age.
Serving as the group's historian and cultural guide, Albert enriches each expedition with stories, folklore, and a wealth of knowledge that only comes from years of experience.
